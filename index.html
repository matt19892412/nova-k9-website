Three programmes, one path.

(01)

Puppy Foundations

Get it right from day one.

For: new puppies finding their feet — the best possible start, before any habits have a chance to set in.

  • Name, focus & early engagement
  • The very start of recall
  • Loose-lead basics
  • Toilet, crate & alone-time confidence
  • Proper socialisation, at the right pace
Four 1:1 sessions · 4 weeks£P.O.A
Flagship (02)

Focus First

Focus first, behaviour second — the foundations every great dog stands on.

For: adolescents, adult dogs and rescues — the puller, the dog that won’t come back, the one that can’t switch off.

  • Focus & engagement with you
  • Lead manners that last
  • Reliable recall
  • Settle work — switching off & relaxing
  • Tailored to your dog, in the real world
1:1 sessions · 4–6 weeks£P.O.A
(03)

Bespoke Course

The deep work — done on solid ground, at your dog’s pace.

For: anything from lead pulling to the sensitive cases — reactivity, nervousness, fearfulness, rescue rehabilitation.

  • A plan built around your dog’s triggers
  • A pace that keeps your dog succeeding
  • Confidence & resilience building
  • Practical, real-life strategies
1:1 sessions · tailored timescale£P.O.A
