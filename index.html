The Programme

Puppy Foundations

A four-week journey to give your puppy the best possible start — engagement, confidence, recall and real-world life skills.

Week 1

Engagement & Focus

Teach your puppy that paying attention to you is rewarding and fun.

Week 2

Socialisation & Confidence

Build confidence and neutrality in the outside world.

Week 3

Recall & Play

Build reliable recall through engagement and reward.

Week 4

Real World Skills

Apply skills in everyday situations with calm and focus.

Includes a workbook, progress tracking and a certificate of completion.
